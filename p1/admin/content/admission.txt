        <div class="text-content">
            <div style="margin-bottom: 16px;">Most applicants exceed in one or more of our minimum requirements:</div>
            <ul style="padding-left: 18px;">
                <li>A Bachelor&rsquo;s Degree in any field</li>
                <li>One year of working experience</li>
                <li>Proof of English proficiency<br />
                    - TU-GET (550 or above)<br />
                    - TOEFL (Paper-based test: 550 or above, Computer-based test: 213 or above, or Internet-based test: 79 or above)<br />
                    - IELTS (6.0 or above)</li>
                    <li>Proof of analytical ability<br />
                        - Passing Thammasat Business School&rsquo;s International Graduate Program Admission Test<br />
                        - GMAT (at least 80% on the quantitative part)</li>
                        <li>Demonstration of creative thinking and leadership potential</li>
                    </ul>

                <div class="text-content-header">Application Process</div>
                <div class="text-content">The IMBA application forms are available through the website <a href="http://gradreg.bus.tu.ac.th" target="_blank">http://gradreg.bus.tu.ac.th</a> only. Any candidate who wishes to apply for the IMBA program must follow the steps below:  							<br />
                    <br />
                    1.    Fill in the application form and submit it online.<br />
                    2.    Send required documents to the IMBA Office by post or by hand  							(See &ldquo;Required Documents&rdquo;).<br />
                    <br />
                    IMBA will announce candidates eligible for interview on the IMBA website <a href="http://imba.bus.tu.ac.th" target="_blank">http://imba.bus.tu.ac.th</a> on May 8, 2014. Interviews are conducted on May 10, 2014 by the IMBA admission committee and alumni recruiting team.</div>

                    <div class="text-content-header">Required Documents</div>
                    <div class="text-content">The following documents must be received by the IMBA Office no later than April 27, 2014. 	
                    <br />
                    <br>
                    IMBA Office, Faculty of Commerce and Accountancy  <br>
                    Thammasat University, Thaprachan Campus, 2 Prachan Rd., Pranakorn, Bangkok, 10200, Thailand
                        <br>
                        <br />
                        <div class="font-bold">1.	Undergraduate Record</div>
                        Provide final transcripts of record verifying that they have received an undergraduate  							degree from a certified institution. Photocopy submissions must be signed and certified.  							Four copies are required. 							<br />
                        <br />
                        <div class="font-bold">2.	Citizen Identification Card (for Thais) or Passport (for foreigners)</div>
                        Four copies are required, signed and certified by you. 							<br />
                        <br />
                        <div class="font-bold">3.	Photographs</div>
                        Four 4 x 5 cm. recent photographs (less than six months)  							are required for student records and filing.  							<br />
                        <br />
                        <div class="font-bold">4.	Proof of English Proficiency</div>
                        Provide either of the following test scores taken within the last two years:<br />
                        - TU-GET: Minimum scores of 550<br />
                        - TOEFL: Minimum scores of 550 for paper-based test, 213 for computer-based test, or 79 for internet-based test<br />
                        - IELTS: Minimum scores of 6.0<br />
                        <br />
                        *For more information on TU-GET, please contact the Language Institute of Thammasat University at T.  (66) 2 223 3758-9.  							<br />
                        <br />
                        <div class="font-bold">5. Proof of Analytical Ability </div>
                        - Passing Thammasat Business School’s International Graduate Program Admission Test<br>
                        - GMAT (if applicable): At least 80% on the quantitative part, taken within the last 5 years
                        <br>
                        <br />
