<p><span style="font-family: Verdana;">&nbsp;As IMBA students, it is imperative that we keep ourselves informed about the current global business trend. There was no better place for us to do this than in China, an economic giant which has gone through tremendous changes in recent years.  </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">During our trip to Shanghai, one of Asia&rsquo;s largest metropolises and an important international business frontline, we learned how start-ups thrived in the country&rsquo;s fast changing environment. &nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">Through the experience of Shokay, a manufacturer of luxury apparel made from yak down, we saw how a social enterprise could transform into a lucrative fashion company.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">At GIGA, the provider of a platform linking construction firms to environmental friendly materials, we came to realize that being a &ldquo;green&rdquo; entrepreneur could be both self-satisfying and profitable. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">We also visited IDEO, an international design firm and saw its acclaimed innovation concept at work. This gave us a new perspective on how we should develop our own business ideas when the time comes. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">After spending a day in Yiwu, a city recognized as the <span class="apple-converted-space"><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%;">&ldquo;</span></span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%;">largest small commodity wholesale market in the world&quot;</span>, we were able to see how China was able to cater to the global demand. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">Moreover, we gained a firsthand experience working with CP, a Thailand-based multinational, by observing one of its retail projects and made recommendation to the executive; a challenging but rewarding experience. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: Verdana;">We also made an excursion to Hangzhou, a city renowned for its scenic beauty, to unwind after all the hard work.&nbsp;</span></p>
