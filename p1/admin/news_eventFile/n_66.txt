<p>BizBarcelona is an entrepreneur and innovation event that was held every year in Barcelona, a beautiful city of Spain. The event lasted for 2 days with more than 1,800 meetings between entrepreneurs, investors and consultants. This year (2011) one of the teams from IMBA, DeepScan, participated as a start-up company to compete in a Global Entrepreneurship Competition with other 23 teams from around the world. DeepScan won the second place.</p>
<div>More than a prize, they had a chance to meet and talk to many venture capitalists that joined the event seeking for interesting project to give funding. They went home full of business cards and emails to follow.</div>
