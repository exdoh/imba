<p>SMILE Floss creates distinguished value in silk supply chain by repurposing pure silk supplied from silk farmer to produce &ldquo;Organic Dental Floss&rdquo;. We are the first organic dental floss producer in ASEAN. <br />
&nbsp;</p>
<p>Organic Dental Floss is made of Thai pure natural silk coated by coconut oil and natural bee wax. With the magnificent characteristic of silk in flexibility and gentle, SMILE floss helps reducing gum bleeding and better than synthesized dental floss.<br />
&nbsp;</p>
<p>Thai silk is considered as one of the world greatest premium quality silks and long-lasting cultural heritage of Thailand. Currently, this cultural is going to be disappeared due to the intensive market competition from overseas.<br />
SMILE Floss&rsquo;s goal is to help Thai silk farmers and create business competiveness for silk farmer families in rural area. We offer alternative sustainable business for more than 2,000 families while preserving Thailand&rsquo;s heritage and native folk wisdom.</p>
