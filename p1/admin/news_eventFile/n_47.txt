<p><span style="font-size: larger">March 27, 2010, IMBA program held IMBA Alumni Party under &ldquo;Colorful Punk&rdquo; theme at King Power Pullman Hotel, Bangkok. The party was filled with joy and laughter of IMBA alumni and current IMBA students, IMBA batch 5 and 6. Firstly this party should be held in December 2009 to celebrate the New Year together among IMBA alumni and current students. Unfortunately, there were many uncontrollable factors so the party had to be moved to the end of March. </span></p>
<div style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt"><span style="font-size: larger">Before the party started, there were many concerns about political issue in Bangkok. Even though many roads were closed but we still continued to have this party to fulfill the objective of bonding and strengthen the relationship among IMBA Family. </span></div>
<div style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt"><span style="font-size: larger">The party started around 7 pm. with Professor Frederic, IMBA director, gave us an opening speech (while wearing big red fluffy hat). The food in the party was very good especially Kao Mun Kai (chicken with rice) and various desserts. In the party, we had many activities such as dressing competition, Fan Pun Thae IMBA Game show, IMBA activities slideshow and lucky draw. Also we had IMBA6 music band played on the stage for the first time, you guys rock!!!&nbsp;The winners of dressing competition were P&rsquo;Ti IMBA#5 and P&rsquo;Gimm IMBA#6, their hair styles and costumes were so cool!. The party ended up around midnight when we all went home with happiness and lots of prizes. In the following morning, many IMBA#6 were late for class 555. </span></div>
<div style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt"><span style="font-size: larger">We will not forget the splendid time at the party. See you again next year <span style="line-height: 115%">J</span></span></div>
