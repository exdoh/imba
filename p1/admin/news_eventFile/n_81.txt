<p>&nbsp;After winning several business plan competitions in 2010-2011 both in Thailand and aboard, Deepcan project has been moving toward actual commercialization.&nbsp; The first operating unit of DeepScan is now completed and being used for clinical study at Chulalongkorn hospital. The clinical study will take one year and the commercialization will proceed after that.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">In October-December 2012, DeepScan participated in True Innovation Awards 2012 which was an innovation competition held annually by True Corp. In this competition, only innovations from Thai innovators can apply to compete to win prize money and various supports from True Corp. From over hundreds of innovations submitted, DeepScan won a Silver Award with the prize money of 200,000 Baht. The prize money will be used to buy more equipment and fasten the clinical study.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Business plan and innovation competitions are just a way to test the plan and receive feedback from experts in related fields. The true goal of DeepScan is always an actual commercialization where we can create real business and real impact to our societ.</p>
