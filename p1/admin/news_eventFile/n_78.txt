<p>&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; ">Both the team and the stadium are the brainchildren of Newin Chidchob, who is a controversial figure in Thai politics. Nonetheless, it is impressive what he has built in Buriram: a quality stadium which could be in Europe, and a successful football team, which has caught the excitement of the province.</span></p>
