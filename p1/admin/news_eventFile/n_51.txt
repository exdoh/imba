<p>In the night of August 4, 2010, IMBA students drove through a heavy pouring rain to Thailand Science Park at Rangsit, a birthplace of innovations. There, Dr. PaladejChaloeykitti, the director of Technology Management Center, gave us an overview of what happen inside this mysterious land. We got a chance to meet with Mr.Paijit Sangchai, CEO of Flexoresearch Group, who shared us his experience in commercializing innovation and inspiration to become an entrepreneur. Next, Ms.SonthawanSupattaraprateep gave us information about a great program called &ldquo;iTAP&rdquo;, Wait! It is not a new product from apple!!!iTAP is an incubator program for private companies that want to improve their product/service or want to become innovative or even want to start up a company. Lastly, Ms.Sumonwan and Ms.Thininut from Technology Licensing Office shared us information about intellectual property right and the upcoming competition, the Idea 2 Product or I2P. After finished at 9.30pm, some went to have a late night meal while the rest hurried back to bed, ready for tomorrow.</p>
<p>By : Mr.Chatchai Aphibanpoonpon : IMBA Class of 2011 (GE Track)</p>
